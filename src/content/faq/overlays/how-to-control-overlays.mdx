---
title: How do I control overlays in Pits n' Giggles from my input device?
category: Overlays
order: 1
---


You can interact with Pits n' Giggles from your input device during a session by using the game's UDP action codes.
Please refer to the [Overlays Setup Guide](/blog/setting-up-overlays#controlling-the-overlays) for more details
